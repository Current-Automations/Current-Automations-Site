Product overview, one script.

A 75–90 second product film for trades owners. Four real scenarios, one quiet system behind all of them. Tone is plain-spoken, grounded, respectful of their time. The owner stays the hero — the automation is the quiet partner in the background.

● Framing note  ·  This is a concept demo of what the system does. "Northstar Mechanical" is a fictional sample business used to illustrate the four scenarios — not a real client, not a case study, not delivered results. All language in the film reads as "here is what the system does," never "here is what we delivered."

06 Production notes

Audio direction

Voice: Canadian male, 38–52. Plain-spoken, never up-selling. Think: experienced shop manager, not a SaaS narrator.

Music bed: sparse. Low piano + soft synth pad. Drops out under stat reveal and during the "you didn't touch the phone" beat. Re-enters under the dashboard.

SFX: single phone-ring loop on the truck scene (clipped on the hard cut). Soft "tick" on each win-chip pulse. Type-bed audio under transcripts is optional — if used, keep it just below perception.

Motion direction

Pace: measured, confident. Most moves are easeOutCubic with 0.4–0.6s durations. No bouncy back-eases anywhere except the win chip pulse.

Transitions: hard cuts between scenarios. No swipes, no spin transitions, no whip pans.

Reveal pattern: eyebrow chip → display headline → subtitle → UI artifact. Each step ~0.2s behind the last. The UI element is always the last thing to appear.

Localization

Voiceover is Canadian English. Sample customer names should rotate: Margaret Reilly Tom Brouwer Anders Kowalski. Sample addresses use Ontario cities the audience knows (Mississauga, Oakville, Burlington, Hamilton). Phone numbers use 905 / 416 / 519 area codes. Currency in CAD with "GST in" callout.

Cut-down variants — playable

60s social cut: drop S3 (quote follow-up), shorten cold open to 6s, trim dashboard to 8s.

30s pre-roll: hard open on stat reveal; one scenario only (S2 missed-call text-back); CTA.

15s bumper: stat reveal → S2 win chip → CTA card. No VO; sound design + on-screen text only.
