# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Owner-operated trades businesses (plumbing, HVAC, electrical, cleaning, landscaping) in Durham Region + GTA, remote across Ontario. A 45-year-old owner who answers his own phone, is skeptical of tech, and responds to clarity over cleverness. He lands here from cold email or a call and decides in seconds whether this is real or another agency pitch.

## Product Purpose
Sell one front-door offer: a free audit that finds where the business leaks revenue (missed calls, slow follow-up, manual admin, intake bottlenecks). The site's job is to get the audit booked. Missed-call recovery is the flagship symptom, not the whole story. Success = "Book Free Audit" clicks and demo-line calls.

## Brand Personality
Trustworthy, direct, quietly confident. Industrial-tool-brand restraint (Basecamp/Shopify in directness, not in look). Plain language, concrete numbers, no jargon. Never leads with "AI"; leads with jobs won and calls answered.

## Anti-references
- Generic AI-agency landing pages (gradient-text hero, hype copy, fake urgency)
- Fake testimonials or invented social proof: banned outright
- SaaS hero-metric template and identical icon-card grids
- Anything that reads "tech startup" instead of "a guy who fixes a real problem for trades"

## Design Principles
- Proof you can dial: the live demo line (1-365-601-7474) is the strongest honest proof; surface it like a product
- Show the flow, don't claim the outcome: missed call → text in seconds → booked job, made visible
- One front door: every page funnels to "Book Free Audit"
- Premium through craft, not noise: depth, rhythm, and typography inside the existing teal/navy system; teal stays scarce
- Write for the truck, not the boardroom

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA contrast everywhere. Visible :focus-visible (2px teal) on all interactive elements. prefers-reduced-motion honored on every animation. Tap-to-call on mobile.
